Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025  |  Saral Rojgar Pvt. Ltd.

Saral Rojgar ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information: name, phone number, email address, location
  • Professional information: skills, work experience, job preferences
  • Usage data: app interactions, pages visited, search queries
  • Device information: device type, OS version, IP address

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To match workers with relevant job opportunities
  • To allow employers to find suitable candidates
  • To improve platform features and user experience
  • To send job alerts and platform notifications
  • To prevent fraud and ensure platform safety

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share your profile with employers when you apply for a job or make your profile public. We may share data with service providers under strict confidentiality obligations.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data. No method of internet transmission is 100% secure, so we encourage use of a strong password.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and download your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work correctly.

7. Children's Privacy

Saral Rojgar is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
