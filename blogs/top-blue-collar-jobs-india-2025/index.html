Top 10 Blue Collar Jobs in India with Highest Demand in 2025

📅 Apr 28, 2025✍️ Saral Rojgar Team⏱ 6 min read
📊

India's blue collar workforce is the backbone of its economy. With rapid urbanisation, infrastructure growth, and the gig economy boom, demand for skilled manual workers is at an all-time high. Here are the top 10 jobs in highest demand right now.

#1

🚚 Delivery Driver / Rider

E-commerce and food delivery platforms have created millions of delivery jobs across all cities.

₹15,000 – ₹30,000/month
#2

🔌 Electrician

Urbanisation and real estate growth keeps electricians in constant demand in every city.

₹18,000 – ₹40,000/month
#3

🏗️ Construction Worker / Mason

Infrastructure projects across India are creating massive demand for construction labour.

₹500 – ₹900/day
#4

🍽️ Cook / Kitchen Staff

Restaurants, dhabas, and cloud kitchens are expanding rapidly in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities.

₹12,000 – ₹25,000/month
#5

🔧 Plumber

New housing societies and apartment complexes constantly need skilled plumbing work.

₹600 – ₹1,200/day
#6

🧹 Housekeeping / Cleaning Staff

Offices, malls, hospitals, and housing societies hire housekeeping staff year-round.

₹10,000 – ₹18,000/month
#7

🛡️ Security Guard

Demand for security personnel continues to grow with rising commercial and residential complexes.

₹12,000 – ₹20,000/month
#8

🏭 Factory / Production Worker

Manufacturing sector expansion is creating thousands of production line jobs.

₹10,000 – ₹22,000/month
#9

🎨 Painter

Real estate boom means constant demand for interior and exterior painters.

₹500 – ₹1,000/day
#10

📦 Warehouse / Packing Worker

E-commerce warehouses are expanding in every major city and hiring at scale.

₹12,000 – ₹18,000/month
