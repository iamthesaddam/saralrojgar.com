About Saral Rojgar

We believe every worker deserves a fair chance and every business deserves reliable help. Our mission is to bridge the gap.

Our Mission

Simplifying Employment
for Bharat

Saral Rojgar was born from a simple observation — millions of skilled workers in India struggle to find work, while thousands of small businesses struggle to find reliable staff. The gap is not about skills or jobs — it's about connection.

We built Saral Rojgar to close that gap. Using technology, we make it incredibly easy for blue-collar and grey-collar workers to showcase their skills and for small & medium businesses to find the right person fast.

Our platform is designed for real India — simple interface, Hindi support, and zero barriers to entry.

🤝 Trust

Verified profiles and honest reviews build confidence on both sides.

⚡ Speed

Find a job or hire someone within hours, not days or weeks.

♿ Accessibility

Simple UI designed for users with basic smartphone literacy.

💰 Fair Pay

Transparent wages and no hidden middleman cuts for workers.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025  |  Saral Rojgar Pvt. Ltd.

Saral Rojgar ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information: name, phone number, email address, location
  • Professional information: skills, work experience, job preferences
  • Usage data: app interactions, pages visited, search queries
  • Device information: device type, OS version, IP address

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To match workers with relevant job opportunities
  • To allow employers to find suitable candidates
  • To improve platform features and user experience
  • To send job alerts and platform notifications
  • To prevent fraud and ensure platform safety

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share your profile information with employers when you apply for a job or when you make your profile public. We may share data with service providers who help us operate the platform, under strict confidentiality obligations.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep it confidential.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and download your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work correctly.

7. Children's Privacy

Saral Rojgar is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Contact Us

For any privacy-related concerns, contact us at: saralrojgar@techrelieve.ai
